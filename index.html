About

A bit about me.

I'm an Embedded Systems Engineer pursuing Industrial Computing and Automation Engineering at the National Institute of Applied Science and Technology (INSAT) in Tunis, Tunisia.

With hands-on experience in firmware development, real-time systems and IoT, I specialize in dependable embedded solutions using STM32, ESP32, FreeRTOS and protocols such as UART, SPI, I2C, CAN and Ethernet.

I'm currently an Embedded Systems Engineering Intern at the Nicolaudie Group (LightingSoft AG) in Meroux, France, building scalable Modbus TCP servers and strengthening CI/CD pipelines for production firmware.
